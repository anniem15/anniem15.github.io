USING DARTFISH FOR VIDEO ANALYSIS

Dartfish is an application that allows for video analysis of sports performance. It's highly useful as it allows coaches to provide direct real-time feedback to athletes about their technique and form.

In the video above, I have performed a video analysis of an individual's long jump performance. I broke down his performance into six key snapshots:

Takeoff Phase: The first and second snapshot showed the initiation and execution of his takeoff. His technique was appropriate as he kept an erect posture, with a slight vertical head tilt as he was preparing to take off.

Flight Phase: The third, forth and fifth snapshot show him using the hitch-kick technique during the flight phase. He maintained a slight vertical head tilt, and his arms were swinging in a controlled manner, pulling his arms overtop his head prior to landing.

Landing Phase: The sixth snapshot showcases his landing. He was able to land with his heels hitting the sand first, which is an appropriate technique.
